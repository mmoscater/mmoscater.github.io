Latitude vs Temperature

As we can see from the graph, there is a significant trend of cities closer to the equator (Latitude of 0) reporting higher maximum temperatures. What one may not expect is that the northern hemisphere (Latitude greater than 0) is reporting much lower maximum temperatures.
